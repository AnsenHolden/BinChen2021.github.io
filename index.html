Bin Chen (陈斌)

I am currently an Assistant Professor with the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen (HITSZ). I got my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Shu-Tao Xia . I have also been fortunate to visit Prof. En-Hui Yang at the University of Waterloo (UW) from Dec 2019 to May 2020. My research interests include Coding Theory and Information Theory, Machine Learning and Deep Learning.

Research Fields

Coding Theory and Information Theory

  • Coding Theory:   分布式存储纠删码;   基于深度学习的信道编译码;
  • Information Theory:   深度压缩感知理论及逆问题;   语义压缩/通信;   有损压缩的率失真理论;

Machine Learning and Deep Learning: Theory and Applications

  • 图像/视频/深度模型压缩:   点对点/分布式通信场景下/多视角的有损/无损图像/视频语义压缩;   深度模型压缩；
  • 图像/视频/多模态检索:   基于哈希/乘积量化的快速检索算法;   多模态/跨模态/细粒度图像/视频检索/检索增强及其应用；
  • AI Security:   Adversarial Machine Learning;   AIGC and its privacy security;   分布式/联邦梯度压缩与隐私安全;

"Theory without Practice is empty; Practice without Theory is blind!"
